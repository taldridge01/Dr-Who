Ace

Ace is a fictional character played by Sophie Aldred in the long-running British science fiction television series Doctor Who.[3] A 20th-century Earth teenager from the London suburb of Perivale, she is a companion of the Seventh Doctor and a regular in the series from 1987 to 1989.[4] Ace appeared in 9 stories (31 episodes), and was the Doctor's final companion in the original run of the classic series.
